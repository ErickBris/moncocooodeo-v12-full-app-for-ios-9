A) Installation

Installation is pretty easy. All you need is to open the Oodeo directory, and open Oodeo.xcodeproj.

B) Configuration - top

You can change all of the Xml source link in this App. You just have to open the directory "Config You App HERE" and open "config.m"

FEED

Link

You have to update 2 Feeds, one for the News and one for the home Projects:

  • URLFluxXmlNEWS - For the News
  • URLFluxXmlHome - For the Home Projects

Structure - NEWS

In the News Xml you have to put a title, a description and a link to an image.

Structure - HOME

In the Home Xml you have to put your Website Url and 4 : a title, a description and a link to an image.

Mail

In the App, the user can send you a mail, so you have to put your email, the mail Subject and the mail Message

  • Mail - You need to put your email adress
  • MailSubject - You need to put the email Subject
  • MailMessage - You need to put the email message

Map

Here we are going to see how configurate the map view !

Annotation

In the Oodeo App you can put a place with latitude and longitude.

  • MapLatitude - For The latitude of your place
  • MapLongitude - For The longitude of your place

Place Text

When the user is going to click on the annotation, he's going to see some text.

  • Title - For The title of your place
  • SubTitle - For The SubTitle of your place
